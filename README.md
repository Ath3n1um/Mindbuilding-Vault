# Obsidian Mindbuilding Vault

Mindbuilding, or mental exercise, is the practice of exercising your mind to build mental fitness. It refers to several scientifically backed mind workouts that make you smarter, less distracted, and less impulsive.

Mental fitness includes mental ability: memory, processing speed, reaction time, reasoning, problem-solving, and reading speed and comprehension.

Mental fitness includes concentration, focus, attention, and other executive functions.

Mental fitness also includes abilities involved in mental control, such as self-control and emotional control.

Mental exercise makes you better at thinking and learning. You learn to focus and pay attention for longer. You get distracted less often. You get better at controlling your emotions and actions. You become less impulsive. That's what it means to build mental fitness.
